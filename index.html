Hey, I'm Seb. I'm a student and rail enthusiast located in the East of England, and this is To The Trains.

I produce videos documenting Great Britain's railways, covering the routes to their rolling stock and everything inbetween. The channel's made a strong start in it's first year, with 20 videos published and over 500 subscribers, and since its launch, I've also delved into podcasting and streaming.

Have a look around, watch a video or two, and perhaps join our Discord server.
