To The Trains

YouTube Channel

Having been inspired by other creators in the community, I decided to try something new by starting my own YouTube channel. "To The Trains" was the resulting product, with its name deriving from the old "To The Trains" signs found across the railway. The TTT logo is actually designed to slightly resemble train tracks. It's my goal to document as much about the railway in the UK (and possibly even beyond) as I can, from the rolling stock to the routes served. This is where I combine all of my skills, from video editing and design to web development, also serving as a portfolio in itself.

Cubed!

Online Convention

Cubed! is an in-game Minecraft convention, formerly known as Minecon Server Edition, which is in support of the UK-based charity SpecialEffect. It has been running since 2012, and in 2019 I was part of a small team responsible for graphics and livestream design/operation for the event. Over $7000 has been raised in total for charity, and I continue to volunteer as a Sr Moderator.
Please contact me for more details if you're interested in the livestream graphics and/or operation.

Draavo

YouTube Channel

From May to September I was the sole editor for Draavo's YouTube channel. He is a transport enthusiast that makes videos and livestreams on Roblox, Minecraft, and simulation games. During my time, the channel hit the 1k subscriber milestone after surpassing 500 subscribers in April. Over 50 videos that I edited for Draavo have been published on the channel, amassing hundreds of thousands of views. This, alongside To The Trains, has been my primary project for 2020.
